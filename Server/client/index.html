About Chatty Cathy

Chatty Cathy is a simple and anonymous chat client that connects people from around the world and allows them to share gossip at an unprecedented rate.

Unlike other apps that may sound similar, Chatty Cathy dispenses with all of the extraneous features that make those apps bulky and instead strips the experience down to its core to ensure that users get the most pure gossip sharing experience.

Our simple, streamlined user interface allows even the most technologically challenged user are to jump right into the action and start gossiping at the touch of a button.

And now, with our random username generator, you don't even have to come up with your own username. By simply jumping into a chatroom, you will be assigned a unique and anonymous handle with which to start gossiping.

For the more discerning user, we do also allow you to pick your own usernames in case that is something you prefer.
